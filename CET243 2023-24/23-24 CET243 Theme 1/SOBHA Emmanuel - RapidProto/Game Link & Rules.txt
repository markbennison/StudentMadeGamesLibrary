Game Rules
Follow complete All the goals for the door to open and dont get killed or touch the Lava or you will die.
Once all the goal has been completed the player can then open the door by colliding against it. 
To pick up the Keys just walk over then and it should dissapear that means it has been collected.
Have fun.

Game Controls

Move right: Keyboard = D
	    Console  = Left Stick/Right
Move Left : Keyboard = A
	    Console  = Left Stick/Left
Jump 	  : Keyboard = Spacebar
	    Console  = Button South
Shoot     : Mouse    = Left Click
	  : Console  = Right Trigger
