The game is called 'Hurry Home!' and is about the player trying to escape from an avalanche wall that is following the player. The player must try to hurry home before they come into contact with the wall.

Game Input Controls:
---
Keyboard: 
-
A - Walk Left
D - Walk Right
Space Bar - Jump
Shift Key - Sprint
-----
Controller:
-
Left Joystick - Move Left and Right
South Button - Jump
West Button - Sprint
