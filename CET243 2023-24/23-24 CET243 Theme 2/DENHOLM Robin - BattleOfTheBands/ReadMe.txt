https://github.com/ThatRobin/BattleOfTheBands

Controls:

Player One:
WASD: change move selection / Move Joystick for rythm game.
Space Bar: choose selected Move / Play Note

Player Two:
Up/Down/Left/Right: change move selection / Move Joystick for rythm game.
Right Shift: choose selected Move / Play Note
OR
Left joystick: change move selection / Move Joystick for rythm game.
East Controller Button: choose selected Move / Play Note
